Want to see Eiffel tower, Colosseum, Golden Gate Bridge, FC Barcelona stadium or other dreamy places but don't like the hassle of booking tickets, accomodations, or even visa processing? Want to have a look to your prospective room rental in another country or taking a small tour to your favorite museum? Choose any interesting place that you want to see and we will facilitate a tour experience right from our webpage. Enjoy the visualized tour and take some good pictures of any spot that you like.

Meet the double robotics which will provide you the visualized tour experience. These robotics will cruise along your favorite place while providing you the visualization of the place. You can control their movement from the navigation panel provided and you can also type in some messages for random people passing by .

FAQ

Is the virtual tour web based?
Yes, you can experience the virtual tour from the webpage itself.
Do I need to have double robotics?
No, but for cases like room viewing, the corresponding person must have double robotics.
